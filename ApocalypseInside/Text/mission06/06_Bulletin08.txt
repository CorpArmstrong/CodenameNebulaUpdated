<P><B>Bones Of The Desposyni</B>
<P>
<P>Exhibit name: Bones Belonging To The Desposyni
<P>Danger level: Low
<P>Date of aquisition: 1960 (arrived on station)
<P>
<P>The mass grave containing bones from:
<P>
<P>1. Exhumed bodies of Virgin Mary's children and Jesus Christ's brothers James, Joses, Simon and 2 sisters. Aquired by the Knights Templar throughout the Crusades.
<P>
<P>2. Mary Magdalene and Sarah the Egyptian. Assumed from Provence in 771 by Gerard, Duke of Burgundy and conserved by the Vatican until Mr. DuBeers ordered the relics to be moved to Ophelia in 1960.
<P>3. French kings of the Merovingian dynasty. Provided to Mr. DuBeers by the Priory of Sion.
<P>
<P>DNA tests conducted on the station in the 80-ies showed undeniable relation of all exhibits to each other as well as Lucius DuBeers, Morgan Everett, Elizabeth DuClare, Stanton Dowd and Bob Page.
