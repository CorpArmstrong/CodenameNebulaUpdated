 1. QUEST FLAGS REFERENCE
================================================================================

 These are the flags that QuestSystem checks to determine the ending.
 Flags are set via flagBase.SetBool() during gameplay.

 --- Flags from earlier levels (carried into L2) ---

 PlayerKnowsChinese        Player has Chinese skill at Master level
 DestroyedDocksEvidence     Player destroyed all evidence in the Docks (Ch6 L1)
 bChoseHostileToArmstrong   Player was hostile to Armstrong in Docks conversation

 --- Flags set during L2 ---

 WongKilledSamantha         Wong killed Samantha during social boss fight
 WongSparedSamantha         Wong spared Samantha during social boss fight
 WongBetrayedMeph           Player convinced Wong to kill Mephistopheles
 PlayerGaveUp               Player gave up during social boss fight
 DaedalusKilledAvatar       Daedalus killed Tantalus' avatar
 HostagesAlive              At least one hostage survived the social boss fight
 SocialBossCompleted        Social boss fight sequence is done
 UndockedL2                 Player undocked Level 2 from Ophelia (Hijack task 1)
 StartedBlueFusion          Player started Blue Fusion reactor (Hijack task 2)
 TookSteeringWheel          Player took the steering wheel (Hijack task 3)
 TimerExpired               The MJ12 arrival timer ran out
 PlayerEnteredInfusionChamber  Player entered the upload tube (triggers map exit)
 MagdaleneUploaded          Magdalene's upload to network completed
 TantalusUploaded           Tantalus' avatar uploaded
 IsGameCompleted            Set by QuestSystem when ending is triggered

 2. L2 FLOW - STEP BY STEP
================================================================================

 PHASE 1: ENTRY
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 1. Player arrives at Airlock (from L1)
 2. Depressurize event
 3. Player reaches Hub
 4. Tantalus listens to JC's hologram
 5. Receives endgame instructions from Page's avatar
    (Page says he is in Mt. Weather)

 PHASE 2: COMMAND CENTER
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 6. Player reaches Command Center
 7. Magdalene comes out and shoots Page's avatar with coil gun
 8. MJ12 (mini crossbows) vs Avatars battle starts
 9. Player opens doors to Avatar Lab and Gravity Lab

 PHASE 3: GRAVITY LAB - WONG & SAMANTHA SUBPLOT
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 10. Player enters Gravity Lab
 11. CHOICE: Player convinces Wong to leave Samantha alone
     OR: Player convinces Samantha to follow Wong
 12. Samantha gives key to Dr. Reed's stateroom
 13. At Reed's stateroom: Player finds video recording of Wong
     -> FLAG: PlayerSawWongVideo = true
     (Proves Wong has his own motives)

 PHASE 4: THE BRIDGE - SOCIAL BOSS FIGHT
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 (See Section 3 for detailed branching)

 14. Player reaches The Bridge
 15. Wong kills MJ12 troop hostage
 16. CHECK: DestroyedDocksEvidence?
     YES -> Mephistopheles orders Wong to spare hostages -> skip to Phase 5
     NO  -> Social Boss Choice 1 begins

 17-20. Social Boss Choices 1-4 (see Section 3)

 PHASE 5: POST-BOSS
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 21. Page congratulates Tantalus
     Says MJ12 will be there in 3 minutes
 22. CHECK: Any hostages alive?
     NO -> Timer begins (3 minutes)
 23. Player speaks with Magdalene

 PHASE 6: AVATAR LAB
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 24. On your way to Avatar Lab, Bob Page infolinks, warns troops
 25. Tantalus meets Ophelia and receives endgame instructions
 26. At Avatar Lab
 27. Daedalus says he opened the door to the Bridge
 28. Ophelia (avatar carrier bot) greets player
     Gives instructions to close the door to Avatar Lab
 29. Magdalene sits on medical bed
     Ophelia (medbot) gives her Reizene
     Scans her embryo — it is dead
 30. Ophelia shows Megan Reed's carcass
     Says she uploaded herself
 31. MagdaleneInsideTube sequence starts

 PHASE 7: UPLOAD SEQUENCE
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 32. Magdalene goes to Upload tube
     Player closes the tube from console with a button
 33. Second tube opens
 34. Player walks into second tube
     Teleports to the same map as Tantalus' human server
 35. Second tube closes
     Tantalus' avatar carcass appears in tube
 36. Tantalus' human server opens
     Human server has 0 health in both legs
 37. Player crawls (can't walk — broken legs)
     Ophelia-medbot can heal his legs
 38. Player comes to his avatar
     Small dialogue with himself
 39. Presses button to start upload of Magdalene and Tantalus' avatar
     -> Dialogue "WalkingInTube" starts
 40. Avatars attack
     Timer starts
     Page's face appears on window screens
     He speaks about how NSF failed at a rebellion,
     that Tantalus gave up and committed suicide,
     JC Denton and UNATCO are on their way to recover the station

 PHASE 8: ENDGAME BRANCHING
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 41. Player enters the infusion chamber -> triggers MapExit
     -> QuestSystem.Update() evaluates flags
     -> Player is sent to the appropriate ending map

================================================================================
 3. SOCIAL BOSS FIGHT (WONG) - DETAILED BRANCHING
================================================================================

 This is a multi-round social encounter similar to the social boss
 fights in Deus Ex: Human Revolution. Wong holds hostages and the
 player must navigate conversation choices.

 PRE-CHECK: Did player destroy all evidence in the docks?
 ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
 │ DestroyedDocksEvidence == true?                             │
 │   YES -> Mephistopheles orders Wong to spare hostages       │
 │          -> Skip entire social boss, hostages live          │
 │   NO  -> Social Boss begins                                │
 └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

 ROUND 1 (Social Boss Choice 1):
 ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
 │ Outcome A: Wong kills Samantha                             │
 │   -> FLAG: WongKilledSamantha = true                       │
 │   -> Continue to Round 2                                   │
 │                                                            │
 │ Outcome B: Wong spares Samantha                            │
 │   -> FLAG: WongSparedSamantha = true                       │
 │   -> Continue to Round 2                                   │
 └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

 ROUND 2 (Social Boss Choice 2):
 ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
 │ Player tries to manipulate Wong                            │
 │   -> Wong kills one of two hostages at random              │
 │   -> Continue to Round 3                                   │
 │                                                            │
 │ Player tries to convince Wong to betray Mephistopheles     │
 │   -> Continue to Round 3                                   │
 └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

 ROUND 3 (Social Boss Choice 3):
 ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
 │ -> Wong kills one of two hostages at random                │
 │ -> Continue to Round 4                                     │
 └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

 ROUND 4 (Social Boss Choice 4):
 ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
 │ Outcome A: Player gives up                                 │
 │   -> FLAG: PlayerGaveUp = true                             │
 │                                                            │
 │ Outcome B: Player convinced Wong to kill Mephistopheles    │
 │            and commit suicide                              │
 │   -> FLAG: WongBetrayedMeph = true                         │
 │                                                            │
 │ Outcome C: Daedalus kills Tantalus' avatar                 │
 │   -> FLAG: DaedalusKilledAvatar = true                     │
 └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

 After Round 4: Count surviving hostages
   -> FLAG: HostagesAlive = (count > 0)
   -> FLAG: SocialBossCompleted = true

 4. ENDING CONDITIONS
================================================================================

 The QuestSystem checks these conditions in order. First match wins.

 ┌─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐
 │ ENDING 1: MUTINY (06_Mutiny)                                      │
 │ ─────────────────────────────                                      │
 │ Condition: Player dies at any time on Level 2                      │
 │            OR specific mutiny path conditions                      │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Description: Gray Goo consumes LA.                                 │
 │ Tone: Dark/chaotic                                                 │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Flags: PlayerDied == true (handled by Chapter06L2 death handler)   │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Map: 06_Mutiny                                                     │
 ├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
 │ ENDING 2: CONSPIRACY (06_Conspiracy)                               │
 │ ─────────────────────────────────────                               │
 │ Condition: Timer expired during the Bridge sequence (Phase 5)      │
 │            Player did NOT complete hijack tasks                     │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Description: Bob Page speaks with Tantalus.                        │
 │              Mephistopheles is taken as a test subject to           │
 │              Versalife and has gone crazy.                          │
 │ Tone: Page wins, bittersweet                                       │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Flags: TimerExpired == true                                        │
 │        UndockedL2 == false (did not attempt hijack)                │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Map: 06_Conspiracy                                                 │
 ├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
 │ ENDING 3: HIJACK OPHELIA (06_Hijacking)                            │
 │ ────────────────────────────────────────                            │
 │ Condition: Player completes 3 timed tasks before timer expires:    │
 │   1. Undock Level 2 from the rest of Ophelia                       │
 │   2. Start the Blue Fusion reactor (hack the keypad)               │
 │   3. Take the steering wheel                                       │
 │   + Timer NOT expired                                              │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Description: Ophelia docks+L1 fall into ocean as a fireball.       │
 │              Tantalus and Magdalene stand on the bridge             │
 │              looking at Mars.                                       │
 │ Tone: Triumphant escape                                            │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Flags: UndockedL2 == true                                          │
 │        StartedBlueFusion == true                                   │
 │        TookSteeringWheel == true                                   │
 │        TimerExpired == false                                       │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Map: 06_Hijacking                                                  │
 ├─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┤
 │ ENDING 4: TRANSCENDENCE / BENEVOLENT DICTATOR (06_Transcend)       │
 │ ─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────       │
 │ Condition: Timer expired during upload sequence (Phase 7)          │
 │            OR player died during upload                            │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Description: Ophelia speaks to the world.                          │
 │              MJ12's conspiracy is exposed.                         │
 │              Tantalus and Magdalene are backed up and can use       │
 │              any avatar they want.                                  │
 │              Real Tantalus died.                                    │
 │ Tone: Sad but hopeful — digital immortality                        │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Flags: TantalusUploaded == true                                    │
 │        TimerExpired == true (from upload timer)                    │
 │                                                                    │
 │ Map: 06_Transcend                                                  │
 └─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

 5. IMPLEMENTATION TASKS
================================================================================

 TASK 1: QuestSystem Configuration                          [CODE]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Fill QuestSystem.defaultproperties with 4 quest paths:
   questPathList[0] = Mutiny flags -> "06_Mutiny"
   questPathList[1] = Conspiracy flags -> "06_Conspiracy"
   questPathList[2] = Hijacking flags -> "06_Hijacking"
   questPathList[3] = Transcendence flags -> "06_Transcend"
 Priority: HIGH
 Difficulty: EASY (just data, code already works)

 TASK 2: Chapter06L2 Mission Script                         [CODE]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Update Chapter06L2.uc:
   - Spawn QuestSystem actor on level start
   - Handle player death -> set flags -> send to Mutiny ending
   - Add timer system (3-minute countdown)
   - Call QuestSystem.Update() when player enters infusion chamber
 Priority: HIGH
 Difficulty: MEDIUM

 TASK 3: Social Boss Conversation (Wong)                    [CONVEDIT + CODE]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Create/update conversation in ConEdit:
   - 4 rounds of choices
   - Each choice sets appropriate flags via conversation events
   - Wong NPC reactions (kill/spare hostages)
   - Hostage tracking (count alive NPCs)
 Priority: HIGH
 Difficulty: HARD (most complex single feature)

 TASK 4: Timer System                                       [CODE]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Implement countdown timer:
   - 3-minute timer starts after social boss / Page congratulation
   - HUD display showing remaining time
   - On expire: set TimerExpired flag
   - Timer affects which ending is available
 Priority: HIGH
 Difficulty: MEDIUM

 TASK 5: Hijack Sequence Triggers                           [MAP + CODE]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Place triggers in L2 map for 3 hijack tasks:
   - Undock trigger (button/switch)
   - Blue Fusion reactor keypad (hackable)
   - Steering wheel interaction
   Each sets its flag when activated
 Priority: HIGH
 Difficulty: MEDIUM (needs map editing)

 TASK 6: Upload Sequence                                    [MAP + CODE]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Implement the upload tube mechanics:
   - Magdalene enters tube 1 (scripted)
   - Player closes tube from console
   - Player enters tube 2 -> teleport to human server area
   - Broken legs mechanic (0 health in legs)
   - Ophelia-medbot healing
   - Avatar dialogue (talk to yourself)
   - Upload button -> WalkingInTube dialogue
 Priority: HIGH
 Difficulty: HARD (complex scripted sequence)

 TASK 7: Level Geometry Fixes                               [MAP]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Fix L2 map geometry issues:
   - Complete unfinished areas
   - Ensure all locations from flowchart exist:
     Airlock, Hub, Command Center, Gravity Lab,
     Reed's Stateroom, The Bridge, Avatar Lab, Upload Chamber
   - BSP rebuild
   - Lighting pass
 Priority: HIGH
 Difficulty: HARD (level design work, editor only)

 TASK 8: NPC Placement & Scripting                          [MAP + CODE]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Place and script NPCs:
   - Wong (social boss)
   - Samantha Reed
   - Dr. Mephistopheles
   - MJ12 troops
   - Avatars (enemies)
   - Magdalene (companion)
   - Ophelia (medbot/avatar carrier)
   - Bob Page (hologram/infolink)
   - Daedalus (voice/AI)
   - JC Denton (hologram)
   - Hostages
 Priority: MEDIUM
 Difficulty: MEDIUM

 TASK 9: Daedalus & Page Infolinks                          [CONVEDIT]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
 Create datalink conversations:
   - Daedalus warns about bridge door
   - Bob Page infolinks during Avatar Lab approach
   - Page's speech during upload sequence (face on screens)
 Priority: MEDIUM
 Difficulty: MEDIUM

 TASK 10: Polish & Testing                                  [ALL]
 ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
   - Test all 4 ending paths
   - Verify flag persistence across save/load
   - Check timer accuracy
   - Test social boss all branches
   - Fix any crashes from null references
 Priority: LOW (after everything else works)
 Difficulty: MEDIUM

================================================================================
 6. DIFFICULTY ESTIMATE
================================================================================

 OVERALL: MEDIUM-HARD

 The QuestSystem code is already built and working.
 The 4 ending maps are already complete.
 The conversation files for L2 already exist (OpheliaL2.con,
 OpheliaL2ArmMagdalene.con).

 The main work is:
   1. Map geometry completion (level design — editor work)
   2. Social boss fight implementation (hardest single feature)
   3. Timer system + hijack triggers (medium code work)
   4. Upload sequence scripting (complex but linear)
   5. Wiring QuestSystem to endings (easy — just data)

 Estimated effort breakdown:
   - Code/scripting:  40%  (Tasks 1,2,4,5,6,8)
   - ConEdit work:    20%  (Tasks 3,9)
   - Level design:    30%  (Tasks 5,7,8)
   - Testing:         10%  (Task 10)

 With the build toolchain now working (cnn compile/test),
 iteration is fast. A focused developer could complete L2
 in approximately 2-4 weeks of part-time work, assuming
 the level geometry issues are not major rebuilds.

 The social boss fight (Task 3) is the hardest piece —
 it requires careful ConEdit work to create the 4-round
 branching conversation with flag-setting events. This
 alone could take a week.

 The QuestSystem wiring (Task 1) and Chapter06L2 updates
 (Task 2) could be done in a single coding session with
 Claude Code assistance.
